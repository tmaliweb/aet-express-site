What We Offer Our Services

TWIC Escort

AET Transportation is authorized and licensed to provide TWIC (Transportation Worker Identification Credential) escort services. We facilitate easy access for contractors and other personnel requiring entry to secure port facilities in the Houston and Galveston area.

Whether you are coordinating a single contractor visit or managing a large crew, AET Transportation handles port access logistics with professionalism and precision. Our TWIC-compliant drivers ensure that all required protocols are followed so that your team gets in and out efficiently without delays. Contact us to discuss your TWIC escort requirements and let us simplify your port operations.

Cruise Transport

From Galveston's world-class cruise terminals, AET provides premium group and private transfers for all major cruise lines including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Celebrity, Disney, Norwegian, and Princess. We track your ship's schedule so you're never late.

Our drivers are experienced in Galveston cruise port logistics and know how to keep your departure running smoothly. We also offer return transfers from the port, so your entire trip is covered from start to finish. Contact us early to secure your booking — cruise dates fill up fast.

Airport Transfers

Reliable, on-time transfers to and from Houston's George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH) and William P. Hobby Airport (HOU). Flight tracking ensures we're ready when you land.

Whether you're traveling solo or with a group, we have the right vehicle for your needs. Our professionally trained drivers will handle your luggage and get you to your destination safely and comfortably. Flat-rate pricing means no surprises — just reliable, on-time service every trip.

Hotel Transfers

Seamless transportation between Galveston and Houston hotels, cruise terminals, and airports. We partner with leading hotel brands across the region to ensure a smooth, coordinated experience from check-in to check-out.

Whether you're arriving for a cruise departure, heading to a downtown Houston hotel, or transferring between properties, AET Transportation makes it effortless. Competitive flat-rate pricing and 7-day availability mean you're covered no matter when you travel.

Funeral Escort

AET Transportation provides compassionate, dignified funeral escort services for processions and memorial arrangements. We work directly with funeral homes to ensure respectful, timely service during life's most difficult moments.

Our drivers approach every funeral escort with the utmost professionalism and sensitivity. We understand the gravity of these occasions and are committed to honoring your loved one's memory with care and dignity throughout the entire procession.

Stadium & Events

Whether it's a Houston Texans game at NRG Stadium, an Astros game at Minute Maid Park, or a Rockets game at Toyota Center — AET gets your group there comfortably and on time. We also serve corporate clients including Shell and Prodeo.

From private rides to large group charters, our fleet is ready to handle events of all sizes. Let AET Transportation handle the logistics so your group can focus on the experience. Book early for game days and major events — availability fills quickly.
