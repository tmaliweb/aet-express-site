What We Offer Our Services

TWIC Escort

AET Transportation is authorized and licensed to provide TWIC escort services for easy access for contractors and other personnel to secure port facilities in the Houston and Galveston area. Our TWIC-compliant drivers ensure that all required protocols are followed so that your team gets in and out efficiently without delays.

Whether you are coordinating a single contractor visit or managing a large crew, AET Transportation handles port access logistics with professionalism and precision. Contact us to discuss your TWIC escort requirements and let us simplify your port operations.

Cruise Transport

Start your vacation the right way with a stress-free transfer to the Galveston cruise port. AET Transportation specializes in comfortable, on-time transportation for all major cruise lines, including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Celebrity, Disney, Norwegian, and Princess. We handle everything from individual bookings to large group transfers, making sure every passenger arrives safely and on time.

Our drivers are experienced in Galveston cruise port logistics and know how to keep your departure running smoothly. We also offer return transfers from the port, so your entire trip is covered from start to finish. Contact us early to secure your booking — cruise dates fill up fast.

Airport Transfers

AET Transportation provides seamless, door-to-door airport transfer service to and from Houston area airports, including George Bush Intercontinental (IAH) and William P. Hobby Airport (HOU). Our drivers are ready to ensure you arrive on time for departures and greet you promptly upon landing — no waiting, no stress.

Whether you're traveling solo or with a group, we have the right vehicle for your needs. Our professionally trained drivers will handle your luggage and get you to your destination safely and comfortably. Flat-rate pricing means no surprises — just reliable, on-time service every trip.

Special Events & Hotel Transfers

AET Transportation provides custom transportation for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions throughout the Houston and Galveston region. We also offer hotel transfers — reliable transportation between hotels, cruise ports, and airports — so your entire itinerary is covered seamlessly.

Whether you need a single vehicle for a wedding party or multiple vehicles for a corporate outing, we offer flexible scheduling and competitive flat-rate pricing. Let AET Transportation handle your transportation logistics so you can focus on what matters most.
