What We Offer Our Services

Airport Transfers

AET Express provides seamless, door-to-door airport transfer service to and from all major Michigan airports, including Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport (DTW), Flint Bishop International Airport, and Gerald R. Ford International Airport in Grand Rapids. Our drivers monitor your flight status in real time to ensure they're there when you land — no waiting, no stress.

Whether you're traveling solo or with a large group, we have the right vehicle for your needs. Our professionally trained chauffeurs will greet you, handle your luggage, and get you to your destination safely and comfortably. Flat-rate pricing means no surprises — just reliable, on-time service every single trip.

Cruise Transportation

Start your vacation the right way with a stress-free transfer to the cruise port. AET Express specializes in comfortable, on-time transportation for all major cruise lines, including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Celebrity, Disney, Norwegian, and Princess. We handle everything from individual bookings to large group transfers, making sure every passenger and every piece of luggage arrives safely.

Our drivers are experienced in cruise port logistics and know how to keep your departure running smoothly. We also offer return transfers from the port back to Michigan, so your entire trip is covered from start to finish. Contact us early to secure your booking — cruise dates fill up fast.

Funeral Escort

At AET Express, we understand that funeral transportation requires the utmost professionalism, sensitivity, and dignity. Our funeral escort service is designed to give grieving families one less thing to worry about during a deeply difficult time. We work closely with funeral homes throughout Michigan to coordinate procession logistics and ensure every vehicle arrives at the right place at the right time.

Our drivers are trained to conduct themselves with quiet respect and compassion. We provide reliable transportation for family members, guests, and dignitaries, ensuring that the final journey of your loved one is handled with the honor and care it deserves. We are available 24/7 to accommodate even the most sensitive scheduling needs.

24/7 Private Car Service

When you need a premium, private vehicle at any hour of the day or night, AET Express is ready. Our private car service is ideal for corporate executives, VIP clients, weddings, proms, special events, and anyone who demands a higher standard of transportation. We maintain a fleet of clean, well-maintained vehicles staffed by professional drivers who prioritize your comfort and punctuality.

Whether you need a one-way trip across town or a full-day driver for a corporate outing, we offer flexible scheduling and flat-rate pricing with no hidden fees. Our 24/7 availability means you can book last-minute without worry. Experience the AET Express difference — where every ride feels first class.
