About AET Transportation

Houston's trusted transportation partner

AET Transportation is committed to offering a tailored transportation experience to all types of lifestyles by providing comfort, reliability, select concierge services and competitive pricing.

Serving the Houston and Galveston, Texas region, our professional drivers are licensed, insured, and dedicated to your safety and satisfaction — whether you're heading to a cruise terminal, airport, hotel, or a special event.

We are proud to be a licensed TWIC escort provider and a trusted transportation partner to the region's leading hotels, cruise lines, stadiums, and corporate clients.
