Serving Houston, Galveston & Surrounding Areas

Welcome to AET Transportation!

Traveling for business or pleasure and want to drive in style to and from the Houston Hobby Airport, George Bush International Airport (IAH), Galveston cruise terminal, or Houston/Galveston hotel? Then AET Transportation is perfect for you!

Our transportation service offers reliable travel and privacy direct express to your location. AET is located in Houston, TX and we offer ground transportation to Houston and surrounding areas, Galveston, La Porte, Bay Port, and Seabrook.

AET caters to small and large groups who need personal car service, SUV's, to larger accommodations such as 15 passenger shuttle vans and wheelchair accessible vans.

Why Choose AET Transportation

Reliable and Insured Vehicles
The principles of our business is to be a fast and safe transportation company while following all safety and government guidelines.
Skilled and Qualified Professionals
We have the fleet of vehicles, trained drivers, and friendly staff to provide the best customer service in the business.
Servicing the Port of Houston
We provide transport service to the crew members of the ships that need to be escorted out of the ports.
Non-TWIC Escort Service
We also have Non-TWIC escort service for truck drivers and other personnel wishing to gain access to the docks.

We offer TWIC Escort services to surveyors, freight forwarders, insurance representatives, or exporter/importers who need to examine cargo being loaded or unloaded at the port. We also provide escorts for crew members that need to sign on or off, detained crew transportation, transportation for medical purposes, and transportation of spare parts for repair or forwarding.
