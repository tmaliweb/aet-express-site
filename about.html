Who We Are

Our Company

Houston & Galveston's trusted transportation partner

AET Transportation is committed to offering a tailored transportation experience to all types of lifestyles by providing comfort, reliability, select concierge services and competitive pricing. Serving the Houston and Galveston, Texas region.

We specialize in TWIC escort services, cruise port transfers to and from Galveston, airport pickups and drop-offs at IAH and Hobby, hotel transfers, and custom transportation for special events. Our team of professional drivers is fully licensed, insured, and dedicated to your comfort and safety.

At AET Transportation, we believe transportation is more than getting from A to B. It's about peace of mind, punctuality, and a genuine commitment to the people we serve. Whether a hotel, a cruise, an airport or a special event, we can simplify your solution.
