Welcome to AET Transportation!

Traveling for business or pleasure and want to drive in style to and from the Houston Hobby Airport, George Bush International Airport (IAH), Galveston cruise terminal, or Houston/Galveston hotel? Then AET Transportation is perfect for you! Our transportation service offers reliable travel and privacy direct express to your location. AET is located in Houston, TX and we offer ground transportation to Houston and surrounding areas, Galveston, La Porte, Bay Port, and Seabrook. AET caters to small and large groups who need personal car service, SUV's, to larger accommodations such as 15 passenger shuttle vans and wheelchair accessible vans.

AET Transportation Service Co, LLC was established in 2007

To meet the needs of customers taking a cruise at The Port of Houston in La Porte, Texas.

Our company is licensed/permitted and insured in Houston, Houston Hobby, George Bush Airport, and the Port of Galveston. AET is certified to do small business with the City of Houston, Metro and the Houston Port Authority.

At AET our mission is to provide all customers with an excellent car service experience. Our company has Port of Houston security clearance to give our customers ease of access to captains and crew members, PHA tenants, stevedore companies, and ship liners, and vessel agents who need transportation to/from various locations within Houston/Galveston surrounding area Ports.

We understand the importance of professional and customer-friendly service and pride ourselves on consistently exceeding our client's expectations. We are dedicated to help clients plan their transportation arrangements with the best possible service and at the most competitive price.

AET has served the Port of Houston as a vendor in La Porte, Texas

For shuttle services to/from Kemah during emergency fog delays which severely impacted the cruise lines ability to serve the customers' needs as well as Bay Port cruise Terminal through paid reservations by the cruising customer as well as same day pick up service. AET currently provides transportation in Houston/Galveston surrounding ports.
