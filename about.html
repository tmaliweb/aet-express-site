Who We Are

Our Company

Houston & Galveston's trusted transportation partner

AET Transportation was founded with a simple mission: to provide tailored, reliable transportation for every lifestyle. From humble beginnings in the Houston area, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted transportation providers — serving cruise travelers, corporate clients, hotel guests, stadium fans, and those in need of compassionate funeral services.

We are proud to be a licensed TWIC escort provider and trusted partner to Houston's leading hotels, cruise lines, stadiums, and corporate clients. Our commitment to comfort, reliability, and competitive pricing has earned us a 5-star reputation across the Houston and Galveston region.
