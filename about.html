Who We Are

Our Company Story

Michigan's trusted transportation partner for over 15 years

AET Express was founded with a single mission: to provide Michigan residents and visitors with the most reliable, professional, and comfortable transportation experience available. Over more than 15 years in operation, we have grown from a small local service into one of Michigan's most trusted names in transportation — serving thousands of satisfied clients each year.

We specialize in cruise port transfers, airport pickups and drop-offs, and compassionate funeral escort services. Our team of professional drivers is fully licensed, insured, and background-checked. We invest continuously in vehicle maintenance and driver training to ensure that every ride — whether it's the beginning of a dream vacation or a solemn final farewell — meets the highest standard of care.

At AET Express, we believe transportation is more than getting from A to B. It's about peace of mind, punctuality, and a genuine commitment to the people we serve. We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, because life doesn't keep office hours — and neither do we.
